# QAZ-Fold-Base
3d printable base to put bumpon or skuf feet on your CBKBD QAZ fold rev1
The USB port will still be accessible and it retains the mostly open design of the original board.

This base can be printed with basically any settings you want provided you have enough room in the slot to slide the legs of the board into place. I printed this on a BambuLab A1 with mostly default settings for PLA. Seam position was on the back corner, with a couple layers of brim for adhesion. 

Fits most stick on feet and each pad is about 10mm x 45mm so you can put whatever adhesive feet you want on. 
Should be a tight friction fit on the board itself, and uses the light angle of the case to hold it in. I can pick mine up from the case upside down
